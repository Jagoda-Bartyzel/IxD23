SCP_1609

What is scp-1609?

SCP-1609 at current time is a pile of scrap fabric, furniture nails, splinters and wood chips. It's previous state was a chair. SCP-1609 is currently being contained in Storage site-08. The room in which SCP-1609 is being kept in has a flower bed and SCP-1609 is currently being used as the mulch for the flowers. There are special precaution taken by the presonnel going into the containment site All personel must wear plain clothing as to not aggravate the SCP, it is observed that SCP-1609 is sentient and has a strong desire to be the best looking, this is why personnel are required to wear plain clothing and comment on how pretty the flower bed and its mulch are, unless they want to aggravate the SCP. SCP-1609 is in the object class: Euclid. This means that the SCP is an anomaly that is is insufficiently understood or inherently upredictable. Currently there is no way to prevent SCP-1609 from breaching containment.
